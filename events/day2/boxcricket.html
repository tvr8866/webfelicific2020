Rules and Regulations

  1. 5 Over Match
  2. Second over and 2nd batting is compulsory of a girl member
  3. Under arm bowling with one tappi compulsory
  4. No ball is considered if above rule is violated.
  5. For wide 2 runs.
  6. For no-ball double runs.
  7. Direct Six means out.
  8. Single handed catch for one tap pi is out.
  9. Six in no-ball is same as not out.
  10. 8 runs to be given if 4 goes on no ball.
  11. Hit wicket equals to NOT OUT only for girls
